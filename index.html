Design4AI Symposium
October 14, 2022
TU Delft, NEXT!Delft

The TU Delft Industrial Design Engineering (IDE) faculty is hosting the premier European event on Design for AI, October 14, 2022. This one-day symposium features keynote speakers, workshops and networking opportunities that will provide insight for those involved in the design and deployment of AI systems and partnerships.

The event will be attended by a range of international researchers including students, designers and engineers from TU Delft including those from TU Delft’s many AI labs. Dutch and international design agencies and industry will also participate.

The symposium will foreground the importance of design in the creation of AI applications and research. We’ll stimulate discussion about AI design methods, strategies, and ethics.
