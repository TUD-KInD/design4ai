Design4AI Symposium
October 14, 2022
TU Delft, NEXT!Delft

The TU Delft Industrial Design Engineering (IDE) faculty is hosting the premier European event on Design for AI, October 14, 2022. This one-day symposium features keynote speakers, workshops and networking opportunities that will provide insight for those involved in the design and deployment of AI systems and partnerships.

The event will be attended by a range of international attendees including students, designers and engineers from the IDE faculty, and other TU Delft researchers involved in AI including those from TU Delft’s many AI labs. Dutch and international design agencies and industry will also participate.
